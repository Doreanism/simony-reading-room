Tomus VIII of the Leonine critical edition of the complete works of Thomas Aquinas, containing the Secunda Secundae of the Summa Theologiae (Questions 1–56), together with the complete commentary of Cardinal Tommaso de Vio Cajetan (Caietanus). The text was established from Vatican manuscript sources and published at Rome by the Typographia Polyglotta under the auspices of Pope Leo XIII. Questions 1–56 treat the theological virtues (faith, hope, and charity) and related vices.
