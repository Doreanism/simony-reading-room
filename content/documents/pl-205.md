Volume 205 of J.-P. Migne's *Patrologiae Cursus Completus, Series Latina*, published in Paris in 1855. This volume contains principally the works of Peter Cantor (Petrus Cantor, d. 1197), precentor of Notre-Dame de Paris, including his major moral-theological compendium *Verbum Abbreviatum*. Additional minor works by contemporary Parisian masters are appended. The text is printed in the standard Patrologia Latina two-column format with sequentially numbered columns.
