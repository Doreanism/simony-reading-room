The second volume of the early-eighteenth-century Antwerp edition of Jean Gerson's *Opera Omnia*, edited by Louis Ellies du Pin and printed at The Hague in 1706. This tome gathers Gerson's writings on ecclesiastical polity and reform, including numerous treatises and sermons connected with the Council of Constance, the Great Schism, and the conciliarist controversies of the early fifteenth century. The text is printed in two numbered columns per page in the Patrologia-Latina style.
