The *Decretalium Collectiones* is the second part of Aemilius Friedberg's standard 1879 critical edition of the *Corpus Iuris Canonici*. It contains five collections: the *Decretales Gregorii IX* (*Liber Extra*, compiled by Raymond of Penyafort at Pope Gregory IX's direction, 1234, the central canon law collection of the medieval Western church); the *Liber Sextus* of Boniface VIII (1298); the *Clementinae* of Clement V (promulgated 1317); the *Extravagantes* of John XXII; and the *Extravagantes Communes*. The main text is set in two columns per page with critical apparatus below, columns numbered sequentially 1–1340. This photomechanical reprint (Graz, 1959) reproduces the 1879 Leipzig (B. Tauchnitz) edition.
