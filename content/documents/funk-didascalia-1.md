Critical edition of the *Didascalia Apostolorum* and *Constitutiones Apostolorum*, edited by Franz Xaver Funk and published in Paderborn by Ferdinand Schöningh in 1905. This first volume presents the texts in parallel columns: the Latin version of the Didascalia (with Syriac Didascalia translation inserted where the Latin is not extant) and the Greek text of all eight books of the Apostolic Constitutions with facing Latin translation, including the Apostolic Canons as Book VIII, chapter 47. The volume opens with an extensive prolegomena on sources and manuscripts, followed by indices of sources, persons, and vocabulary.
