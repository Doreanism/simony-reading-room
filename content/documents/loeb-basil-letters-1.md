Bilingual Greek/English edition of the letters of Saint Basil of Caesarea (c. 329–379), Volume I (Letters I–LVIII), translated by Roy J. Deferrari of the Catholic University of America for the Loeb Classical Library. Published by William Heinemann, London, and G. P. Putnam's Sons, New York, 1926. The volume opens with a substantial introduction by Deferrari surveying Basil's life, theological controversies, and epistolary corpus, followed by the Greek text and facing English translation of the first fifty-eight letters.
