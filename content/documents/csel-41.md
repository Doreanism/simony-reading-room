A critical edition of fourteen works of Saint Augustine of Hippo — *De fide et symbolo*, *De fide et operibus*, *De agone christiano*, *De continentia*, *De bono coniugali*, *De sancta virginitate*, *De bono viduitatis*, *De adulterinis coniugiis* (2 bks.), *De mendacio*, *Contra mendacium*, *De opere monachorum*, *De divinatione daemonum*, *De cura pro mortuis gerenda*, and *De patientia* — edited by Josephus Zycha. Published as Volume 41 (Section V, Part III) of the Corpus Scriptorum Ecclesiasticorum Latinorum series by the Vienna Academy of Sciences.
