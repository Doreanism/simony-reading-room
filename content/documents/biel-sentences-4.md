Commentary on the Fourth Book of Peter Lombard's *Sentences* by Gabriel Biel (c. 1425–1495), the leading German nominalist theologian of the late fifteenth century. Book IV treats the sacraments (baptism, confirmation, the Eucharist, penance, extreme unction, orders, and matrimony) and the last things. This 1501 Tübingen edition, printed by Thomas Anshelm, was prepared for publication by Wendelin Steinbach, Biel's disciple and colleague at the University of Tübingen, who completed and edited the work after Biel's death in 1495. The text was lectured and disputed at Tübingen and draws extensively on Peter Lombard, Thomas Aquinas, Bonaventure, Scotus, Ockham, and Durandus.
