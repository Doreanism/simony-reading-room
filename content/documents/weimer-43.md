Volume 43 of the Weimar Edition (*Kritische Gesamtausgabe*) of Luther's works, edited by O. Reichert and published by Hermann Böhlaus Nachfolger in 1912. Contains the continuation of Luther's Lectures on Genesis (*In Primum Librum Mose Enarrationes*), covering chapters 18–30, delivered at Wittenberg University from 1535 to 1545 and representing one of his most extensive exegetical works. The lectures combine close philological commentary with theological exposition, engaging patristic sources and scholastic interpreters throughout. Also includes supplements and corrections for volumes 42 and 43.
