Commentary on Books Four and Five of the Decretals of Gregory IX by Nicolaus de Tudeschis (Panormitanus), published at Lyon in 1546. This is the second half of a single bound volume; the [first half](/documents/panormitanus-decretals-3) contains Book Three. Book Four covers marriage and betrothal. Book Five covers criminal matters including accusations, simony, Jewish law, usury, and related topics. The text is set in two columns of Gothic type with marginal glosses. This copy is from the Getty Research Institute.
