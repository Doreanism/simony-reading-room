Commentary on Book Three of the Decretals of Gregory IX by Nicolaus de Tudeschis (Panormitanus), published at Lyon in 1546. This is the first half of a single bound volume; the [second half](/documents/panormitanus-decretals-4-5) contains Books Four and Five. Book Three covers the life and conduct of clerics, prebends, tithes, religious houses, and related matters. The text is set in two columns of Gothic type with marginal glosses. This copy is from the Getty Research Institute.
