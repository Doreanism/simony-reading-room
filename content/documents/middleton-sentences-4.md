Commentary on the Fourth Book of Peter Lombard's *Sentences* by Richard of Middleton (Ricardus de Mediavilla, c. 1249–c. 1308), Franciscan theologian. This 1591 Brescia edition, printed by Vincentius Sabbius, was prepared by Ludovico Silvestrio of Sant'Angelo in Vado, who collated earlier editions, corrected errors, and added conclusions and cross-references to each question. Book IV covers the sacraments, ordination, matrimony, penance, and last things.
