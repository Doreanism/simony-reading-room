So shall you bring a blessing upon yourselves and yours — yes, upon this whole Land and Church — extending even to posterity, whose generations shall (we trust) sing of the memorable acts of this Parliament, in every way (as we pray) most prosperous and happy. And your humble suppliant shall daily pray to God so to bless this your happy Assembly, that thereby God’s glory may be advanced, religion propagated, the commonwealth established, Antichristian heresy extirpated, and yourselves blessed in your deed. Amen.
