A most inexpiable and heinous sin indeed, which only the Pope can absolve. We will close this with what is attributed to Saint Augustine, Sermon 37, *Ad Fratres in Eremo* (though those Sermons are acknowledged to be none of his): *Qui Sacramentum emit vel Ecclesiam, vel Prebendas, vel Ecclesiarum introitus, vel seculari potentia hoc pro se procuraverit, sciat quod cum Gehezi et Iuda iam condemnatus est, iam Leprosus factus, iam de Templo Domini expellendus est* \[He who buys the Sacrament, or a Church, or a Prebend, or his admission into the Church, or has procured this for himself by secular power: let him know that with Gehazi and Judas he is already condemned, now become a leper, and now to be expelled from the Lord’s Temple.\]
