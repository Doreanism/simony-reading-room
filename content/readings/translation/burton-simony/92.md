Fourteenthly, he who owes his tithes cannot deny them on the pretext of any debt owed to him, because tithes admit no such compensation, nor any appeal. In a word, all withholders or defrauders of tithes, in whatever manner — whether it be sacrilegious or simoniacal — are the sons of perdition, notwithstanding any custom, usurpation, or forcible occupation; they are in a state of damnation, and all who take part with them, whether by counsel, aid, consent, or defense, directly or indirectly; they are all of them thieves and robbers of God’s goods, pillagers and despoilers of the saints, church-robbers, and excommunicate persons, excluded from the benefit of prayer, remission of sins, and all benefit of the Church; and without restitution before death, no receiving of the sacrament, no confession of sins, no absolution of the priest will do them any good — but with Judas they are traitors and robbers of God’s treasure. They are infidels and ungracious, ungrateful persons, and shall be eternally damned in hell. And as the Philistines were punished with mice and locusts, with hemorrhoids and manifold miseries, so long as they detained the Lord’s Ark — so shall defrauders of tithes be continually exercised with punishments and grow old in miseries, until they make full restitution. For such are well worthy to be smitten with divine vengeance, to be punished by God’s own judging hands — whether with wicked children, or to die a sudden death, or to be slain, or to have their house and goods consumed with fire from heaven. Such is the reward of sacrilegious simony, or simoniacal sacrilege. But he who uses no fraud or deceit in his tithes shall be rewarded by God with eternal life, which the Father, Son, and Holy Ghost vouchsafe to grant to us. Amen. So he. From which we may observe how detestable the sin of sacrilege and simony is — although I do not otherwise approve of every particular passage cited by my author, as some of them smack of the Romish cask. But let it suffice to make such sins the more odious to all lovers of the truth.
