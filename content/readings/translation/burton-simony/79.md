### CHAP. XIV.

### Of the Censures and Penalties of Simony and Simoniacs.

Let us now come to observe the censures and penalties that have been generally imposed upon simony and simoniacs. Aquinas, following his ancients, calls simony *haeresis quaedam* \[a kind of heresy\]. Gregory calls it the prime heresy in the primitive Church. *Altare vel Decimas, vel Spiritum Sanctum emere vel vendere, Simoniacam Haresin esse nullus fidelium ignorat*: To buy or sell the Altar (as the sacrificer does his Mass), or tithes, or the Holy Ghost — there is none among all the faithful who does not know this to be simoniacal heresy. Irenaeus
