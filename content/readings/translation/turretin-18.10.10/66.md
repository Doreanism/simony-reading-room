# Question X.

Where was our Church before Luther and Zwingli, and by what means was it preserved?

X. Nor do we appear to be evading the Question, as though it were a matter of indifference to us — we are able to answer it directly. The Question admits of a fourfold aspect, or looks to four things. First, the doctrine and faith of the Elect, who constitute the Church. Second, their persons and condition. Third, the place where they were. Fourth, the form of government under which they lived, and the external worship they observed.
