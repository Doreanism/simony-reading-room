If \[a rescript is issued\] while a suit is pending, such authority shall not hold unless a copy of the sentence pronounced or of the proceedings is denied to all parties universally.

If it begins and sets out the name of the one who petitioned or caused \[the rescript\] to be petitioned, per the Code: *Rescripsi*.

If a part of the bull or charter is \[illegible\] separated \[or damaged\], as the decretal on \[illegible\].
