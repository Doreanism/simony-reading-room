### XVIII.

Similarly, concerning those who swear everywhere by creatures and by their \[household\] utensils and the like — the same decree says they are to be most severely punished. Solution. However much one swears over a created thing, believing it to pertain to the Creator, and however much those who swear by some member of God’s humanity sin mortally — whether through blasphemy against him or through scandal to \[those who hear\] — a trifle \[such swearing\] leads to confusion. In such a case the canon says the cleric is to be regarded \[as deserving deposition\] and the layman excommunicated. Hence those who swear in this way are to be most severely punished by the Church.

### After this the question arises

how oaths are to be understood — namely whether according to the intention of the one swearing, or in accordance with the intention of those \[who administer\] the oath, which is received according to the intention of those who swear. Gregorius attests this at dist. xxii, q. v, chapter “cumque,” where he says that whatever \[two parties\] have sworn — whether for good or \[ill\] — we say this is to be received as the one who swears understands it, for one becomes guilty in two ways: \[first,\] through taking the name of God in vain, and \[second,\] through deliberate fraud. If Gregorius in the same chapter appears to wish to say the contrary, in the chapter “humanis” he says that human ears judge such words as they outwardly sound.

### Judicial divination: such words the ears hear and

weigh on both sides, because one ought not to consider only the words but also the intention. The same is held at dist. xxxviii, chapter “Sedulo,” and dist. xxii, q. v, \[another\] chapter, where the objection is raised about the one who believes he is swearing upon the Gospel but swears upon a stone, and about the condemned who swear that they will obey the consuls; they are bound in all matters contained in the documents \[they swear upon\], and believe the texts to contain \[true\] letters, yet the texts written for them are fraudulent
