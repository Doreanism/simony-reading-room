Are you not then inflamed with zeal for God? Does your heart not burn within you, and does a fire not blaze in your meditation, so that you may pursue their wicked deeds with your good deeds, and cut off from them the occasion for their shameful trafficking, by which your reputation is harmed and a stumbling block is placed before the weak? Have mercy therefore, and have compassion, and show men that you are not seeking an easy livelihood in idleness, but that through the narrow and straitened life of this calling you are seeking the kingdom of God. Your cause is the same as the apostle’s: to cut off occasion from those who seek occasion, so that those who are suffocated by their stench may be refreshed by your good fragrance.

## XXVIIII. 37.

We do not bind heavy burdens and lay them on your shoulders, burdens we ourselves are unwilling to touch with a finger. Inquire and take note of the labors of our occupations, and in some of us also the frailties of our bodies and such a long-established custom of the churches we serve as to leave us no room to be free for those works to which we urge you.
