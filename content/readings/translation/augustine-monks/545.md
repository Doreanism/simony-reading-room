## IX. 10.

And he returns again and in every way commends, over and over, what is lawful for him and yet what he does not do. “Do you not know,” he says, “that those who work in the temple eat what belongs to the temple? That those who serve at the altar share in the altar? So also the Lord ordained that those who proclaim the gospel should live from the gospel. But I have made use of none of these things.” \[1 Corinthians 9:13–15\] What is plainer than this? What is clearer? I am afraid that perhaps, when in my discourse I try to expound it, I may obscure what shines and cries out by itself. For those who do not understand these words, or who pretend not to understand them, understand my words far less, or at least confess that they do not understand them — unless perhaps they quickly understand mine because they are permitted to mock them, whereas the same license is not permitted with the words of the apostle.
