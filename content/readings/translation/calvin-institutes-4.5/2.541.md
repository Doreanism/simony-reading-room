# CHAP. V.

The ancient form of church government has been utterly overthrown by the tyranny of the Papacy.

It is now worth our while to set before our eyes the order of governing the Church that the Roman see and all its satellites maintain today, together with the full image of that hierarchy they forever have on their lips, and to compare it with the order we have described from the early and ancient Church — so that from the comparison it may become clear what sort of Church those people possess who, on this title alone, rage against us to oppress us, or rather to crush us.
