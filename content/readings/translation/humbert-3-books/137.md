## HERE BEGINS THE SECOND BOOK.

### I. What the blessed Apostle Peter and his fellow apostles thought about heretics.

Having undertaken, to the best of my modest ability, to defend against persistent detractors the judgment of Peter, prince of the apostles, pronounced against Simon Magus with the zeal of that dove \[the Holy Spirit\] whose son he was, I judged no more fitting beginning than the words of the same man whose distinguished faith has never failed nor ever will fail.
