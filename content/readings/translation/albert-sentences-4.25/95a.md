## H. Pope Alexander on those who are ordained under compulsion by simoniacs or heretics.

Likewise, when Pope Alexander decrees that simoniacs are altogether to be condemned and deposed, one must understand this as admitting an exception: unless someone has been drawn in by force. For concerning such men, and concerning those who have been ordained under compulsion by heretics of any kind, Innocent says that they can have some pretext of excuse, provided they immediately depart from them and renounce their wicked conventicle.

## ARTICLE VI.

## Why are sellers of spiritual things called simoniacs, when in fact they do not follow Simon but rather Gehazi?

1. Since sellers imitate not Simon but rather Gehazi, why are they called simoniacs?
