On this part many authorities are likewise adduced in the Text.

The Master, for the resolution of these matters, introduces four opinions, which are known to anyone who inspects the Text. But among the moderns there is no doubt about this: that by heretics, whether still within the Church or even existing outside it, all the sacraments are conferred, provided that they are priests or bishops, or stand in the other orders in which they are able to minister sacraments, and that they preserve the form and the intention of the Church.
