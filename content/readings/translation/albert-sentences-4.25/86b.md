2. Again, through a channel not joined to the spring nothing flows from the spring; but a heretic who has been cut off is like a channel not joined to the spring; therefore nothing descends through him; therefore neither does order.

3. Again, no wise person works toward the destruction of his own work; but if a heretic, as is said, were to confer the sacraments of the Church, this would be to the destruction of the Church; therefore the Author of the Church does not perform that work; but the Author of the Church performs every effect of a sacrament; therefore through heretics who have been cut off he performs nothing; therefore they confer nothing.

4. On this same point many authorities are adduced in the Text.
