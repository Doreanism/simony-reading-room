He also says that they cannot give the Holy Spirit, whom they themselves have lost; that those ordained by heretics have their head wounded; that one who has lost honor cannot confer honor; and that such a man has received nothing, since in the one giving there was nothing which he could receive. He further holds that, to those who come over from the heretics by the imposition of hands, only lay communion is to be granted, and that none of them is to be promoted to any clerical honor, however slight. Gregorius likewise says that the consecration of the Arians is sacrilegious when communion is received from their hand.
