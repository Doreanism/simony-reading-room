this simoniacal \[practice\] has been perpetuated year after year in one deceived \[house\], and in another the devil’s stratagem has made it yet more simoniacal, claiming it as a debt most justly owed under the law of God, as though from the law of Lucifer’s prescription. For they establish for themselves as law this maxim of the devil: that the most wicked simony acquires the force of law through prescription or long-standing custom — as if it were said that ancient malice, perpetuated by the members of the devil, prevails by prescription against Christ. This argument is evident from particular chronicles and from the absence of many counter-instances. But in the fourth blasphemous development, upon which God does not rest, the pope claims for himself the collation of all the benefices of the Church under the law of original foundation and from the whole patrimony of the Crucified; and from this flows the venality of benefices, dispensations, rights, and dispensations, so that the correction of this crime surpasses every possibility of human correction, because by nature the sinews of Behemoth’s loins are twisted by God \[cf. Job 40:17\].

Now I conjecture three remedies from the grace of God. The first is that God illuminate the mind of the pope — Urban VI, for example — so that, giving heed to the papal law, *C. 1 q. 3 c. fin.*, he shatter all simony of this kind. But that would be an unexpected and immense miracle. Therefore the second remedy is nearer to probability: that secular lords, informed by the Lord, should harmoniously resist the pope and obstruct the fountain-head of simony, and take into their own hands the alms of the fathers with which the simoniacs trade in this way, since it is necessary that the fire of the devil burn continuously so long as it has tinder to consume.
