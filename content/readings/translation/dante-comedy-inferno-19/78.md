Of you shepherds the Evangelist took note,
when she who sits upon the waters
was seen by him to play the whore with the kings;

she who was born with seven heads,
and drew her authority from ten horns,
so long as virtue pleased her husband.

You have made yourselves a god of gold and silver;
and how do you differ from the idolater,
save that he worships one, and you a hundred?

Ah, Constantine, of how much evil was mother —
not your conversion, but that dowry
which the first wealthy Father took from you!”

And while I was singing him such notes,
whether wrath or conscience bit him,
he kicked out hard with both his feet.

I truly believe that it pleased my leader,
so contentedly did he keep listening
to the sound of the true words spoken.

Then he gathered me in both his arms;
and when he had drawn me wholly upon his chest,
he climbed back up the way by which he had descended.

Nor did he tire of holding me close to him,
until he carried me over the summit of the arch
which crosses from the fourth to the fifth bank.

Here he gently laid down his burden,
gently because of the jagged and steep crag
which would have been a hard crossing even for goats.

Then another valley was disclosed to me.
