77. Hence an action that gives rise to an obligation of restitution must be contrary to justice strictly understood, and not merely contrary to charity.

78. To this injustice, injury is opposed.

79. To charity is opposed ill will, whether it be formal — arising from a direct intention — or virtual, \[imputed\] by interpretation.

80. To this ill will belongs unjust discord, which, if it breaks forth into separation — especially in matters pertaining to religion — it is properly called Schism.
