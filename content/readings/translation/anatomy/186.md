I have shown before how heinous a crime simony is, and I tell you truly, it will prove a cursed merchandise to you to sell away a spiritual charge for human favor, or bribes, or money, or any matter of worldly interest. Consider — alas! what do you sell? Even the gift of God which he has put into your hands, not to purchase worldly benefit for yourselves, but glory to him: and how will he take it at your hands, if you thus abuse your trust and serve yourselves of him?
