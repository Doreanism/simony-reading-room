And that not only the buying or selling of the gift of the Holy Ghost, but also of other sacred and spiritual things which have affinity with it, is here condemned and comes within the category of Simon’s sin, may sufficiently appear from the text; and that in two ways.

First, it may appear from the ground and reason upon which the Apostle condemns this purpose of Simon Magus, which was that he thought the gift of God could be purchased with money.
