And therefore the canons of the Church have been very strict in debarring such persons from the ministry of holy things, because they cannot say with the Apostle, *I thank Christ Jesus our Lord — that he hath counted me faithful, putting me into the ministry* — for Christ has not put them in, but they have thrust themselves in, and so are no ministers of his.

The case standing thus, it may give just occasion to inquire what effect then such a one’s ministry can have upon the people.
