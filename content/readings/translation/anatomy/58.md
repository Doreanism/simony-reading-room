### 3rdly

The third form of bribery by which the guilt of Simony may be incurred in deed is *ab obsequio* — by homage or service. Thus, whoever either at present, or by any obligation for the future, renders service or homage, or by any good offices ministers to the interests of a man with the design of procuring his own advancement to a spiritual office, is guilty of Simony as the buyer; and likewise as the seller is he who, on that account, promotes him thereto. For, while by such service the one bribes and the other is bribed, it is altogether the same as if the transaction had been made with money. When Jacob purchased his wives from Laban by his service, they considered themselves as sold for money. And so the homage or service that one pays for spiritual things is nothing other than the money
