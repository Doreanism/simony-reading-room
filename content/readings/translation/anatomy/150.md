For, since the priest’s ministry is the instrument for conveying all the blessings that are annexed to the administration of the evangelical ordinances, what can be thought but that God has a controversy with that people whom he gives over to the conduct of those from whom they can derive none of these blessings? But in this case, as various persons within the same flock may hold different persuasions about it, so must we cautiously distinguish and differentiate them according to their various sentiments. There may be some to whom the simony of such a pastor is notorious and known, but who reflect upon it with grief and regret. There may be others who know it, and yet willingly comply with him.
