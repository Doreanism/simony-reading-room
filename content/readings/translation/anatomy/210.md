### Fourthly

It would be most necessary that you should revive something of the ancient discipline against simoniacal patrons. You know the ancient canons struck with *anathema* those lay persons who transacted in such execrable bargains. And truly, nothing could give simony a more mortal wound than to bring under your censures the *sellers* as well as the *buyers* of spiritual offices, and to exercise the spiritual weapons of the Church equally against both.
