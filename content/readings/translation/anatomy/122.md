## CHAP. V.

*Whether those who enter by Simony are to be regarded as Ministers of Christ?*

What is here proposed is a question whose resolution determines the satisfaction and tranquility of conscience for both priest and people, pastor and flock, with respect to their respective concerns in the administration of ministerial duties.
