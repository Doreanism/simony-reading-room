### Fourthly

Consider by what instinct you are moved to design yourself for this holy office. Those whom God calls, he stirs up by the secret instinct of his blessed Spirit, through which he works in them such divine motions of fervent charity and zeal for his glory and the salvation of souls, that they are thereby driven to embrace the greatest hardships and to resolve to sacrifice their own ease, quiet, and carnal interests, in a calling wherein they may serve God to these ends.
