Wherefore, from such clear premises, we cannot but conclude that for a right and sufficient ordination to constitute a man a true minister of Jesus Christ and endow him with a lawful ministerial power, there must be, together with the external instrument, a concurrence of the internal principal efficient — as it was in the sending out of Barnabas and Saul, who received their power and authority from God, and the external seal thereof from the Church; the Holy Ghost gives the command and calls them, and their brethren gave them imposition of hands.
