## CHAP. III.

### *How Simony Is Practiced in the Administration of a Spiritual Office.*

The sin of Simony does not confine itself within the compass of those transactions by which men make their way into the holy calling of the Church; it extends also to the exercise of that calling in the hands of many who bear charge.
