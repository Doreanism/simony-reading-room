Nor can those pastors who, for gain, basely make merchandise of the spiritual things committed to their dispensation, expect the reward of the *crown of glory* from the hands of the chief Shepherd, while, in order to the obtaining thereof, they discharge not their duty on the right principle or according to the rule prescribed by the holy apostle Peter, saying, *Feed the flock of God which is among you, taking the oversight thereof, not by constraint, but willingly; not for filthy lucre, but of a ready mind*.
