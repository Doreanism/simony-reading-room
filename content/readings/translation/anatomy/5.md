But on behalf of the Christian Church, and with a view to that purity and holiness which the Lord was to require in his spiritual Temple, which was immediately to be established and to continue to the end of the world. Wherefore, Origen, expounding these words, says: *Mystice autem Templum Dei est Ecclesia Christi, &c.* By the Temple of God is mystically meant the Church of Christ; and by these three sorts driven out thence by Christ are designated three kinds of covetous persons, who are to be accounted unworthy to be in the Church and communion of the saints.
