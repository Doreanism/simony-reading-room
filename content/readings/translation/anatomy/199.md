How has the climbing of some into the Church by way of monetary transactions opened the mouths of adversaries, to reproach us as thieves and robbers who enter not by the door nor have any call from God; and as hirelings who serve for purchased benefices? Ah! What a scandal does it breed among many Christians? And how does it render the holy calling contemptible generally among the common people, and even among those who otherwise hold thoughts of veneration for it, when they see it prostituted to commerce and made a matter of buying and selling?
