And thus are those guilty who make use of the Word of God, the Sacraments, their preaching, exhortations, admonitions, consolations, prayers, benedictions, and the acts of discipline, censures of the Church, or any matter of the pastoral office, as the profitable commodities of their shop — setting them forth or shutting them up according as they may best contribute either to their profit or to their praise. For not only does he play the simoniac who sells the Gospel for money or material bribes; but also he who preaches to purchase applause and the praise of men.
