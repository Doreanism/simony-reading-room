## CHAP. IV. On the Heinousness of the Sin of Simony.

If the heinousness and impiety of this sin of *Simony* were seriously considered, it could not fail to beget a perfect detestation and abhorrence of it in the heart of any true Christian. Having shown what *Simony* is, and the several ways by which guilt of it may be incurred, I proceed now to set it forth in its own colors, and to show how great and heinous a crime it is.
