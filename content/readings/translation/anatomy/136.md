If, for the resolution of this inquiry, we take the opinion of Gregory the Great, he is very confident in concluding the absolute ineffectiveness and invalidity of such a person’s ministery, saying: *Quicunque Sacros Ordines vendunt & emunt, Sacerdotes esse non possunt, Unde Scriptum est, Anathema Danti, Anathema Accipienti, &c.* “Whosoever sell and buy holy orders cannot be priests; hence it is written, *Cursed be the giver, cursed be the receiver*: This is simoniacal heresy. How then, if they are themselves a curse and not saints, can they sanctify others? And, seeing they are not in the body of Christ, how can they either deliver or receive his body?
