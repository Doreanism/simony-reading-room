That it is so, we may easily conclude from the severe anger of God against David on this account, 2 Sam. 12, because he had given great occasion to the enemies of the Lord to blaspheme. And so indeed do those who practice Simony and make merchandise of spiritual things — they give occasion for blaspheming God and for reproaching him with partiality, as if he had more respect for the rich as more capable of his gifts and graces than the poor, and as if his Church were a shop or warehouse where those who have the most money are best served.
