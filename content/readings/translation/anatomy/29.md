That the buying or selling of any of these things (save those of the last kind) falls under the sin of *Simony* is scarcely doubted by anyone. But as for the matters of benefices, church livings, and things set apart to serve spiritual offices, there are many whose interests prompt them to raise objections on that subject; and because these things are in their own nature temporal things, they boldly argue that all such things, together with their rights, presentations, and titles thereto, may be bought and sold, and that at least the buying or selling thereof does not fall under the guilt of *Simony*.
