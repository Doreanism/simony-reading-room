watch. And he gives a caution to all, to avoid such men as those who *serve not our Lord Jesus Christ, but their own belly; and by good words and fair speeches deceive the hearts of the simple*. So also the apostle *Peter* warns against such, saying, *There shall be false teachers among you, who privily shall bring in damnable heresies*, etc. *And through covetousness shall they with feigned words make merchandise of you; whose judgment of a long time lingers not, and their damnation slumbers not*. And what character the apostle *Jude* gives of such men may be read at length in his epistle.
