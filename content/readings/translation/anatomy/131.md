For, while he comes thereto not by any divine call or warrant, but intrudes by money and bribes, the Holy Ghost does not concur to confer the spiritual power and authority. And therefore of such a one says Ambrose, *Per pecuniam acquisivit indebitum Ordinem, & Deum perdidit in interiori homine* — “by money he has acquired orders he had no right to, and he has lost God in the inward man.” And he shows at length how no grace or good is conferred by such an ordination.
