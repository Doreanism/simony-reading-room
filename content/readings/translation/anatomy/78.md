All such practices are nothing else but gross Simony, while they sell the truth and make merchandise of souls — whether the price be present profit, the hope of future gain, human favor, fear, ambitious designs, or vainglory; for all these are the currency of *Simon Magus*. Of such cheating merchants in the Church the apostles have sufficiently warned us, giving them their proper characters. The apostle *Paul*, speaking of such men, calls them *unruly and vain talkers, and deceivers* — teaching *things which they ought not, for filthy lucre’s sake*.
