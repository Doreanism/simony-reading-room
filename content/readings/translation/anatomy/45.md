And whereas this may be done either by real money, or by other things measurable in terms of money, or by what may serve one as well as money, *Gregory* the Seventh and the schoolmen therefore observe that the sin of *Simony* may be committed in the effect or deed in three ways (which are comprehensive of all others): *Aut munere à manu, aut à Lingua, aut ab Obsequio* — by a gift either by the hand, or by the tongue, or by homage or service.
