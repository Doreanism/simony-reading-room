It was on this account that it was said of Hophni and Phinehas that the sin of the young men was very great before the Lord, because they gave occasion for men to abhor and set light by the offering of the Lord. And so do those who by their simoniacal bargains make merchandise of spiritual things: for they give such scandal by setting these things up for sale as causes the multitude to hold them in lesser esteem, and to account them worth no more than the price laid out for them, or the visible benefit reaped from them, and less worth than those temporal things which cost them more dearly; nay, and in the end, altogether to contemn and despise them, reckoning them as matters of no moment, but only as artifices of gain and advantage.
