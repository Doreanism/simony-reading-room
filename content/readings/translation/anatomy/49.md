“and is not free of Simony.” For, as the Canonists rightly say, *Remissio Juris*, etc. — “A passing over of that right which should accrue to one by virtue of a presentation, is as much as to give something.” And who does not know that in such cases *Remissio juris proprii cadit sub rationem pretii* — “A man’s yielding up of his own right is of the same nature as a price paid for what he transacts about.” The Council of Turon condemns the advancing of any person to the government of churches *Sub annuo pretio* — for a yearly rate — as making the priesthood vendible.
