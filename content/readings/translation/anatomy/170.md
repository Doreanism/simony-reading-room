Nay, and in hundreds of texts the Lord in Scripture sets this before the eyes of those whom he calls and sends forth, thereby to engrave upon their hearts the sense of the charge whose burden they are to undertake, that they may count the cost before they begin the work. And therefore, when the Lord told Ananias that he had called Saul as a chosen vessel to bear his name before the Gentiles, he said withal, *I will show him how great things he must suffer for my name’s sake*. So that, if you are called of God, you will have a deep sense of the weight and burden of the ministry upon your spirit, and will look upon it as a work that requires much watching, and fasting, and prayer.
