Do not mistake me, dear brethren, as if it were my design to reproach young men, or by reflecting upon others to purchase an opinion of the innocence of my own past youth from those infirmities and errors that are incident to others — nay, I desire rather to be humbled in the sight of God for the least error or irregularity to which I am sensible that the folly of my youthful affections has betrayed me.
