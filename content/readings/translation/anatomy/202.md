*First,* much deliberation and circumspection is required in granting licenses for young men to go out and preach the Gospel. For, since the Apostle has given warning *to lay hands suddenly on no man*, this being the first step toward the imposition of hands, and an act which puts them in some capacity for ordination, it is not to be done hastily either.
