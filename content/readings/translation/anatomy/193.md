And next, it is requisite that you make use of such means as may afford you sufficient information and the best advice for fixing upon a person most suitable for the charge. Reputation may do something herein; your own acquaintance with and knowledge of the gifts and qualifications of men may do much; but if you scruple, or dare not, or cannot rely upon your own judgment, then have recourse to the counsel and advice of others.
