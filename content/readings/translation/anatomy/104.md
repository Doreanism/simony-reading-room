Therefore, those who sell away these inestimable gifts, or in any way prostitute them to merchandise for any temporal thing in exchange, are guilty of the most high ingratitude and of heinous contempt of Jesus Christ, as undervaluing the pledges of his love and setting them at a more disproportionate rate than exists between the smallest farthing and the most precious jewel that the richest mines of this world can yield; and they therefore deserve to forfeit his love, whose gifts they have held in so little regard.
