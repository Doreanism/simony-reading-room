*First,* how great and heinous a sin *Simony* is may appear by the following reasons.

### 1st, Because *Simony* is a kind of theft, and of a high nature at that.

We justly account him a thief who, being a steward and entrusted with his lord’s substance, given charge to dispense it for the provision and maintenance of his household, and authority freely to fill all the offices in the family with worthy and faithful fellow-servants — if he should so abuse this power and trust as either to embezzle his master’s goods, or convert them to his own use, by selling offices and placing men in charge for bribes,
