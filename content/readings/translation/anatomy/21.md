Thus things set apart and dedicated to holy and spiritual uses, and for the support and maintenance of spiritual offices, together with the rights and titles thereto, though in their own nature they are temporal things, yet by reason of this connection to spiritual things they become sacred and spiritual. And these things God lays claim to and seals as his peculiar *gifts*: thus the Lord calls the oblations, tithes, firstfruits, and devoted things set apart for the priests and Levites *his gifts*, saying expressly more than once, *I have given them to you.*
