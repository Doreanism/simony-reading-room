And surely, whoever is touched with this sense, and seriously considers what a weighty charge it is, will never make use of the silver keys of Simon Magus to open a passage for himself into it; nay, were it not for the obligation of a call from God, he would not stoop to take it up. It is only a delusory fancy of carnal minds — which look upon it as an office destined for ease, pleasure, and profit — that makes men run to purchase it with money.
