### *Secondly,*

As for those to whom the simoniacal entrance of such a one into the ministerial charge is known, and who yet readily comply with him: not only shall his administrations prove ineffectual to such persons, but by their consent and compliance they involve themselves in the guilt of his sinful practices, inasmuch as they have fellowship with the unfruitful work of darkness and, seeing his spiritual theft, give their consent to it — a sin evidently condemned by both the Psalmist of Israel and the Apostle of the Gentiles.
