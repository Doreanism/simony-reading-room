wit: either by obtruding falsehood and error for truth to their own advantage and gain, ministering corrupt rather than wholesome food to the people — selling as genuine what is only adulterated and counterfeit; or by making the true and holy ordinances of Jesus Christ vendible in their administration, setting these spiritual things up for sale.
