The people see him clothed with orders and authority from the Church, and set over them as their pastor, from whose hands they are invited to expect those spiritual benefits which God has promised to convey to souls by the ministry of the Gospel, in those divine ordinances. Now, if he is the lawful minister of Jesus Christ, all is secure enough on both sides: he may confidently exercise the acts of the evangelical priesthood, and by a good title administer the sacred things belonging thereto; and they may confidently look for the gracious effects of his ministration.
