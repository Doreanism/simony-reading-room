It is then to be considered that the guilt of *Simony* may be incurred either by a mercenary entrance into a spiritual office, or by a mercenary exercise or administration of spiritual things within that office. As to the entrance upon a sacred or spiritual office, men may incur this guilt either as principal agents, or as abettors and instruments therein, participating with them.

Those who come first under our consideration are the principal agents. A man as such may incur this guilt in three ways: either *in affectu*, in the inward affection; or *in conatu*, in the endeavour; or *in effectu*, in the external effect or deed.
