*-que insidias pertimiscamus:* “There is an end of episcopal authority, and the sublime and divine power of governing the Church, if it has come to this pass that we fear the threats of evil men and the snares they may lay for us.” But I hope the fear of these things from men will move you neither to shrink from your duty nor to comply with them in sin; since you know that your Lord and Master has so often warned you *not to fear men*; and that you will find much more comfort and quiet in your conscience when, to please God, you displease man, than when, by gratifying man, you displease God. And there is nothing that will discourage simoniacal designs more than when men see you careful
