Thus, when any person purchases a presentation and title to a spiritual office or ecclesiastical charge, or procures ordination thereto, or obtains votes for his election, by money or any gift or bribe whatsoever, given by himself or by others on his behalf; or when any patron bestows a presentation, or any bishop or ordinary gives ordination, or any man gives his voice in an election to such an office, upon the condition of receiving any money, gift, or bribe — such persons are in effect guilty of palpable simony.
