*Secondly,* another means for remedying this evil is to revive something of the ancient discipline of the Church against it, and to inflict upon those found guilty of the sin of *simony*, either *in conatu aut effectu* — in endeavor or in deed — censures proportionate to their guilt. This rod of ecclesiastical censure is that scourge which your Lord and Master has put into your hands, whose example should stir up your zeal to drive the buyers and sellers out of the Temple.
