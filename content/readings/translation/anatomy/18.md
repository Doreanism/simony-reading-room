And that it may appear that these, by reason of this relation, are more particularly the gifts of God and therefore not subject to buying or selling, it is to be considered that sacred and spiritual things are such either *ex sua natura*, of their own nature, or *ex institutione Divina*, by God’s ordinance and appointment.

Some things are spiritual of their own nature; such are all the graces and gifts of the Holy Ghost, whether ordinary or extraordinary: and these are called the gifts of God because, as his peculiar gifts, they are given and bestowed by the Spirit of God according as in his wisdom he is pleased to dispense them for the good of his Church.
