For, how shall they preach, says the same Apostle, except they be sent? And therefore, although God has been pleased — in order to prevent the confusion that vain pretensions to a divine call might introduce into his Church — to appoint an ordinary way for the confirmation of his own call by the seal of ecclesiastical ordination, yet when that seal is procured from the hands of men where the call is wanting from the hands of God, it is of small significance: it is but the setting up of a man in an office in the household without the master’s consent.
