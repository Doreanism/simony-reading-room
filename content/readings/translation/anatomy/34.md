*Thirdly*, with good reason I may say that whoever buys or sells a benefice, or a presentation to it, buys and sells the office as well: for it is a well-known maxim, *Causa causae, est causa causati*: “That which is the cause of the cause, is also the cause of the effect.” Now the money which the buyer pays to the seller is the cause that procures the presentation; the presentation procures ordination, and ordination the office; and so the money answers for all; and being the cause of the presentation, is also the cause of the office, which without the presentation is not attained.
