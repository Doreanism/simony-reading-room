For the Lord will have none to serve him in his house but those who are called by himself. And therefore the Apostle Paul has told us that no man takes this honor to himself, but he that is called of God, as was Aaron. And it is this that he so often pleads as the authority of his own ministry — that he was called and set apart of God thereto.
