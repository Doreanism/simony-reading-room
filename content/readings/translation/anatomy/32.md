“It is most holy unto the Lord.” So that whosoever transacts by way of buying or selling for benefices, or any part of the church livings or stipends dedicated to the ministry of spiritual offices, cannot escape incurring the guilt either of *Simony* or *Sacrilege*. If it is done merely in contemplation of the benefice, without regard to the office, but only so that the buyer may enjoy the church revenue without any purpose to exercise or discharge the office to which it is annexed, then whoever buys or sells a benefice in such a manner becomes guilty of gross *Sacrilege*, in that he disjoins and separates what God has joined together, and falls under the rebuke and curse uttered from the mouth of the Lord through *Malachi* against those who rob God, saying, “Will a man rob God?
