I proceed next to show how the guilt of Simony may be incurred by others, as instruments and abettors thereof. For those who are thus partakers in it, by walking contrary to the rule of the Apostle who has said, *Have no fellowship with the unfruitful works of darkness, but rather reprove them*, become *socii criminis* — companions in the crime and guilt. And thus men may be guilty in several ways.

First, a man may incur this guilt by promoting a simoniacal design, by becoming a mediator or secret agent — whether by advice and counsel, by transacting between the parties or on behalf of either of them in any condition, compact, or payment of money, or by using any sinister devices to that effect.
