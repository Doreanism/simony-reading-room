### *First,*

To those who know that a pastor has conveyed himself into their charge by simoniacal means, and are grieved that they should be given over to so hopeless a ministry, I say it highly concerns them to search into the cause of God’s controversy with them. And it is likely they shall find it to be such as provoked God of old to give over his people to the like judgment — to the seduction of false and treacherous prophets, and of covetous and careless, cruel and foolish shepherds and watchmen — namely, because they despised his Word and were disobedient and rebellious to the voice of his faithful messengers, as may appear from Micah, Zephaniah, Zechariah, and others of the prophets.
