For this has the approbation of the Apostle, who says, “If a man desire the office of a bishop, he desireth a good work.” And so his desire is good if it proceeds, as *Gregory* says, *A zelo Charitatis Divinitus excitato* — from a zeal of charity stirred up by God. And for any to comply with the desire of such a person, upon consideration of his worth, is neither simoniacal nor sinful.
