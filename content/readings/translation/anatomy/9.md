My first work then must be to show what *Simony* is. I trust it is known to most who speak of it that this sin of *Simony* derives its name from Simon Magus; yet we must not therefore suppose that it had its first beginning from him, but that we read him to have been the first author thereof in the Christian Church, and to have been guilty of this sin in an eminent degree; and upon this account it is that it derives its denomination from him.
