How greatly connivance or forbearance of sin renders a man guilty may appear from the Lord’s threats against those who would *hide their eyes from the man who giveth of his seed to Molech*, and against Eli for conniving at the wickedness of his sons, and against the church of Thyatira for *suffering Jezebel*. Wherefore, for any man to be privy to a person’s simoniacal ways — whereby that person studies, as a thief, to creep into a charge of the church — and not to represent this to those who by their authority should obstruct him, is by his connivance to become guilty of fellowship with him in his sin.
