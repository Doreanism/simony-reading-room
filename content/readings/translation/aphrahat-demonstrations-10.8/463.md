what I have now been able to take from that inexhaustible treasure I have sent to you; and although I have dispatched it to you, all of it nevertheless remains with me. For the treasure is never diminished, since it is the wisdom of God; and its dispenser is our Lord Jesus Christ, just as he himself testified, saying: “All things have been delivered to me by my Father” \[Matthew 11:27\]. And while he is the dispenser of wisdom, he is also Wisdom itself, as the Apostle says: “Christ the power of God and his Wisdom” \[1 Corinthians 1:24\]. This is that Wisdom which has been distributed among very many, and yet in no way diminished, just as I showed you above—that the prophets received of the Spirit of Christ, and yet Christ is in no way reduced.
