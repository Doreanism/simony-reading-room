# The Meaning of This Twenty-Fifth Distinction, in General and in Particular

## It Is Also Customary to Ask Whether Heretics \[Can Confer Orders\]

Above, the Master determined \[the doctrine\] concerning the sacrament of orders; here he determines concerning those who administer the sacrament and those who receive it. He divides \[the treatment\] into two parts. First he inquires concerning heretics: namely, whether they can ordain. Second, concerning simoniacs in particular.
