# X.

Simon, however, first encountered me, Peter, in Caesarea Stratonis — where the faithful Cornelius, though a Gentile, came to believe in the Lord Jesus through my ministry — and attempted to pervert the word of God, with the holy brethren present alongside me: Matthew, formerly a tax-collector, and Barnabas, and Nicetas and Aquila, disciples of Clement, bishop and citizen of Rome, who had also been trained under Paul, our fellow apostle and co-worker
