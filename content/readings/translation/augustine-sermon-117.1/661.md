# SERMON CXVII.

On the words of the Gospel of John 1:1–5, “In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God,” etc. Against the Arians.

## CHAPTER ONE.

1. The Word of God as it is to be understood; at what price it is to be purchased. The passage of the Gospel that has been read, dearly beloved brethren, calls for the pure eye of the heart. For we have received our Lord Jesus Christ — through his divinity, as the one who established the whole of creation; through his humanity, as the one who restored fallen creation — as proclaimed by John the Evangelist.
