# DISTINCTION XXV

## Chap. I. On Those Ordained by Heretics.

256 A question commonly arises: whether heretics who have been cut off and condemned by the Church can confer holy orders upon returning to unity, and whether those ordained by them ought to be re-ordained upon returning to the unity of the Church. The words of the Doctors, who seem to disagree considerably, render this question tangled and nearly insoluble. For some appear to hold that heretics cannot confer holy orders, and that those who seem to have been ordained by them receive no grace.
