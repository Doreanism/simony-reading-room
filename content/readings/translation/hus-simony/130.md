And that assembly is full of uncleanness, of unclean birds such as owls and horned owls — that is, of men who do not love the light of Christ, like bats, who know how to fly in the darkness of sins but do not know how to fly in the light of Christ, for they do not advance in virtues.

Further, Saint John says that all peoples have drunk the wine of the wrath and fornication of that Babylon — that is, from every human generation, such as: virgins, widows, wives, young men, widowers, and husbands. Some of them have consented to that fornication — that is, to departing from Christ; for in Scripture every mortal sin is called fornication, since by it the soul departs from her spouse Christ toward the devil. Further he says: And the kings of the earth — that is, those who rule the earth — they too commit fornication with that assembly; and the merchants of the earth — that is, the covetous — have grown rich through the power of that assembly; for that wicked assembly raises up the covetous and especially the simonists. From their participation the faithful must guard themselves; for immediately afterward Saint John says: “And I heard another voice, saying: Come out of her — that is, out of Babylon — O my people, and be not partakers of her sins, that you receive not of her plagues; for her sins have reached unto heaven, and the Lord has remembered her iniquities.”
