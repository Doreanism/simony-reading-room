but when the merciful Savior healed a sick man on the Sabbath, they counted it a sin. The Savior proved to them that they showed greater care for a donkey than for their neighbor. O, how many there are who are more diligent about cows, oxen, sheep, swine, money, and horses than — I will not say other men’s souls — but even their own souls! May God grant that all turn earnestly to the salvation of their own souls, and be diligent also for the salvation of others!
