And how heavily the sons of Mammon will receive this teaching! For they have fallen far from Christ’s commandment, who says: *You have received freely, give freely*; and from the instruction of Saint Paul, who says: “Having food and clothing, let us be therewith content. For they that will be rich fall into temptation and a snare of the devil, and into many foolish and hurtful lusts, which drown men in destruction and perdition; for the love of money is the root of all evil, which while some coveted after, they have erred from the faith and pierced themselves through with many sorrows. But you, O man of God, flee these things.” Thus Saint Paul spoke to Timothy the priest, and so also to us.

Therefore you, O man of God and priest, beware of covetousness in every direction, and especially in matters of the spirit.

But the poor priest says: I am a curate and I have nothing but my keep; how am I to clothe myself, and how am I to buy books so that I may be able to preach?
