And though a thousand thousands were with him to whom God’s commandment does not fit, nor humility, nor gentleness, nor chastity, nor love — they all hold the losing side.

### \[M\]

And that we are to measure closeness to the Lord Christ in this way, He Himself says: \[Matthew 12:50\] “Whosoever does the will of my heavenly Father, he is my brother and my mother and my sister.” And again He says: \[John 15:14\] “You are my friends, if you do the things which I command you.” Behold how He measures closeness to Himself by the keeping of His commandments!
