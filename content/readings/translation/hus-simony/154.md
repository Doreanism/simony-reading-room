### \[N\]

Therefore let us follow Him, let us obey Him, and let us build on Him by faith, hope, love, and all good works; let us look into Him as into a mirror and let us strive toward Him with all our powers. And let us hear that He says: \[John 14:6\] “I am the way, the truth, and the life” — the way in example, the truth in promise, and the life in reward. The way in example, so that whoever walks it shall not go astray; the truth in promise, so that what He has promised He will fulfill; and the life in reward, so that He Himself will give Himself to be enjoyed in eternal joy. He is also the way, in that He leads to salvation; He is the truth, in that He gives light to the faithful in their understanding; and He is eternal life, in which all the elect shall live forever in joy. Toward that life, and by that way and truth, I wish to go and to draw others!
