she came to him in his house, the prophet said to her: “Come here, come here, wife of Jeroboam! Why do you disguise yourself? Behold, I am sent to you as a hard messenger. Go and tell Jeroboam: Thus says the Lord God of Israel: Because I exalted you from among the people and made you a leader over my people Israel, and I divided the kingdom of the house of David and gave it to you; and you were not like my servant David, who kept my commandments and followed me with all his heart, doing what was pleasing before my face, but you have done evil above all who were before you, and you have made yourself foreign and molten gods, to provoke me to anger, and you have cast me behind your back. Therefore, behold, I will bring evil upon the house of Jeroboam, and will cut off from the house of Jeroboam everyone who pisses against the wall” — that is, every lad and dog — “and the imprisoned \[1 Kings 14:10\] and the last in Israel; and I will sweep away the remnant of the house of Jeroboam, as a house is swept until it is clean. For those of Jeroboam who die in the city, the dogs shall eat them; and those who die in the field, the birds of the air shall devour them: for the Lord God has spoken!” — Behold, Scripture thus shows why the Lord destroyed King Jeroboam and his people: as it came to pass, because he made two golden calves for the people to worship, forsaking God’s commandment which says, “You shall have no other gods”; and secondly, because for money he gave the priesthood over those calves.
