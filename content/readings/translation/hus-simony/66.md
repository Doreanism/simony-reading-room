those who entered their dignity wickedly, and are able to relinquish it, are thieves and robbers — and as long as they do not restore it, they hold it wickedly; therefore St. Gregory says that if he does not resign before death, and death does not find him in harsh penitence, he will without doubt perish forever. May the Lord God grant that those who are stained by simony may truly repent!
