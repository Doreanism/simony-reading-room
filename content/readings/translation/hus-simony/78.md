Therefore, having so good a brother and so gracious a father, all faithful Christians love one another by his commandment, and they share the same spiritual nourishment — his holy body — and the same drink, his holy blood; and all are clothed in the same garment, namely love, and they dwell in the one house, which is the holy Church;
