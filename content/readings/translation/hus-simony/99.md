And the more vainly and extravagantly one wastes his soul, the more torment of eternal fire he shall suffer — if he does not repent.

And how will those priests excuse themselves who disgracefully waste church endowments on fornication, furnishing their fine concubines, mistresses, or mates more handsomely than the altars and images in the church — giving them gowns, cloaks, and fur coats, and taking the tithes of the poor and their offerings! And so they have already made their fornication into a custom, keeping wives without shame, some hypocritically calling them sisters — and will sisterhood excuse fornication? as both Scripture and custom show.

### \[K\]

And there is no one who would vigorously lament this great calamity! The elders do not take care of it, for they themselves would first have to abandon the same; the lords take no heed, some living the same way themselves, and others out of carnal favor; and some, even if they are grieved, think they are not obliged to reform it; and some who begin fall away — on the one side fearing summons before the courts, on the other side because the prelates immediately release offenders; and then the parish community either takes no heed, or does not dare, or does not know how to stand resolutely against manifest wickedness.
