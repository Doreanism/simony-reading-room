And this communion is so ordained by God that if anyone should wish another son of God to be excluded from that communion, he himself would immediately be deprived of his share in it. Therefore, a faithful Christian takes care to fulfill God’s commandments, and thus to be a partaker in all Christian brotherhood. And there is no need to take money and letters from anyone for this, for he has the assurance from Christ; and the more he loves the Father and Brother, Christ, and fulfills his commandments, the more he partakes of the communion of saints.

### \[M\] But this holy communion is not made known to the people

because few preach about it, and also because the brotherhood devised by men has been greatly extolled out of greed. And so that brotherhood has grown to such a point that however many crafts there are in a city, they all want to have their own brotherhoods. And I hear of one brotherhood among parish priests and noblemen, who have been drawn into it, such that gathering together at one man’s house one day, then at another’s the next, and so on in turn, the brothers eat to excess, get drunk, and gorge themselves, and take no thought of the poor, miss masses at home, and upon returning from the brotherly feasts, do not sober up for several days!
