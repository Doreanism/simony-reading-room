To this I say that every pagan and every Jew who obstinately holds error contrary to Holy Scripture is a heretic; but whether he dies in heresy or not, I commit to God. And so when it is said that only he is a heretic who was previously a Christian, that is not true. Furthermore I say that everyone excommunicated by God, and thus cursed by God, who stands obstinately in that excommunication and curse, is a heretic: for he is cut off from God and from his holy Church, even if before men he may shine as a saint; and conversely, many who before men are excommunicated and accursed are, before God, great saints.\[7\] Therefore let us guard ourselves against errors; let us not call our neighbors heretics rashly or out of malice, if we wish in the end to be blessed!
