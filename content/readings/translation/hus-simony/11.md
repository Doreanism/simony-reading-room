### \[B\]

The third heresy is simony, and it is wicked consent to the exchange of a spiritual thing for a non-spiritual thing, of which there will be further discussion. And know that these three kinds of heresy are not completely separated from one another; rather, one draws after it another. Yet the heresies are distinguished as follows: apostasy is specifically turning away from the law of God; blasphemy is the defaming of divine faith; and simony is the heresy by which the simonist seeks to disrupt the order of God.
