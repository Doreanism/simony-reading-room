And so the devil sifts simony among the people and will multiply it until the end of the world; for the prophet Elisha said to Gehazi: “Leprosy shall cleave to you and to your offspring forever.” And the offspring of Gehazi are all those who do not fully and not purely for God’s sake, but for other reasons, give churches — such that they receive gifts: some \[receive\] honor, some carnal favor, and some money or other things —

### \[E\]

such that monks have given churches to unworthy persons, for whom secular lords or friends have interceded — this according to the will of Lucifer, wishing to be held in honor. Then according to the will of the flesh they gave \[churches\] to their own kinsmen, those who were unfit for the governance of the people of God; and according to the will of the world, they gave \[churches\] to their servants however unworthy, provided only that the servant had lived according to their will, or was willing to give a larger pension — that is, a larger annual payment. And so, when \[in their giving\] they ought to have served God the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit, they served instead the devil, the flesh, and the world.

And so you now know how simony runs in the acquiring and distributing of churches among members of religious orders; now let us see how it runs in the squandering of \[church\] estates.
