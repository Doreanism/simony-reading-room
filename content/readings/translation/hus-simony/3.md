# On Simony

## CHAPTER I.

### \[A\]

Because simony has so multiplied — so that the faithful might condemn it, guard themselves against it, and also so that any simonists might repent — with God’s help I wish to write a little book on simony. And because simony is heresy, and because the wicked count the good as heretics, I therefore wish, as a warning and confirmation for the good and as a correction for the wicked, to set down first what heresy is called, so that people may recognize whether those whom they name heretics truly are so, or whether they themselves are struck down by heresy.
