O Christ! It will be no easy thing for proud priests to enter into such humility — to submit themselves to correction by the community when they are guilty, as You submitted Yourself, being innocent!

### \[F\]

And that kings are especially bound to drive simony from the holy Church, the merciful Savior showed by His own example: when the people of Jerusalem welcomed Him as a king, saying, “Blessed is the king forever, the Son of David!” and spread their garments before Him as He rode on a little donkey, He then entered the temple and vigorously drove out the traders from the temple, \[Matthew 21:12\] who had sinned by commerce in a more concealed and lesser degree than those who now sin by simony. And by that act He gave kings an example to drive out simonists and wicked priests, who harm the kingdom far more than any external enemies or robbers; nor can the kingdom stand firm in peace so long as simony stands within it.
