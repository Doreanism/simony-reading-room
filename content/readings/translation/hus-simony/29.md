Thus it is set down in the books of established law.

But alas, none of this is now taken into account. Yet there will come another time in which it will be taken into account, when they come to know their own damnation. And since this heresy has multiplied greatly, it is therefore well to open the eyes of simple folk, that they may recognize simonists and guard themselves against them. And it is fitting to begin from the head and examine it — whether it is defiled with this leprosy — and then to look to the other members; and perhaps it will be found that from head to foot throughout the body leprosy is poured out.
