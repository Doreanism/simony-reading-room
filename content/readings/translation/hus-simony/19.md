For whatever bishop sells the gift of the Holy Spirit — though he still\[28\] shines before men in episcopal robes — is already deprived of the priesthood before the eyes of God. Therefore the holy constitutions also curse simoniacal heresy and command that those who seek money for the giving of the gift of the Holy Spirit be deprived of the priesthood.
