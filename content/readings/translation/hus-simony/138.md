Therefore it is necessary for a man to pray to God diligently and often, that he may be pleased to guide him in such works; and it is also necessary to drive away sins that might stand as an obstacle, and to look to Scripture and await the sweet inspiration of the Holy Spirit.

The second election is called imprudent, and is when someone chooses something according to human ordinance, without regard for God’s law. And by this election popes, bishops, and other prelates are commonly chosen. And according to those ordinances which have no foundation in God’s law, there is commonly deficiency.
