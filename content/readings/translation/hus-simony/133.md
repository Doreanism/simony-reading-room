And by that same Spirit, Saint Peter appointed Saint Clement.237) And so the apostles in general appointed bishops and priests; and when revelation was not granted to them, they put forward two, Matthias and Joseph, and cast lots, so that the Lord God might show which of the two he had chosen — and the Lord God showed Matthias.238) And so if a community were not granted revelation as to which one God would be pleased to have, then with good intent, without carnal attachment, they should choose several good men for the bishopric, and then pray the Lord God earnestly that he would be pleased to show whether any of them were worthy; and if not, that he would be pleased to point out another. But, alas, they push themselves forward on their own account, and the people are carnally drawn toward acquaintances, or toward relatives, or toward the proud; and there is also no great faith and hope in God that he would give them a worthy spiritual ruler.
