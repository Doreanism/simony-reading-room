Thus Absalom by his fair Tongue, bribed the Affections of the Men of Israel, and by stealing away their Hearts thereby, ensnar'd them in a course of Rebellion and Treason against his Father. And thus; by the Peoples Tongues, was Saul brib'd to spare Agag, and the fat Cattel of the Amalekites, and so was ensnared in the sin of Rebellion against God; fearing, lest if he should not have hearkned to their Voice, he should have lost their Favour, and incurr'd their Hatred: Therefore said he to Samuel, I feared the People, and obeyed their Voice.
