As by the 4th Council of *Toledo*, They are condemn'd as unworthy of the Priestly Office, *Qui Honorem illum muneribus obtinere motiuntur* : " Who do but endeavour " to obtain that Honour by Gifts. And the *Canonists* determine, That *Sola promissio est Simonia*; The Promise alone is *Simony*: and that no Offer, or Paction, or Condition in Spiritual Things, is free from this Vice.
