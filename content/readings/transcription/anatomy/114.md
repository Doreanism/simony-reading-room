But having given this short hint, I shall proceed rather to shew, how, by the Judgment of the Catholick Church, and joint Suffrages of many Fathers together, Simony hath been accounted a Crime deserving, and Laws and Canons have been made for inflicting the highest Censures upon Persons guilty thereof; who, according to their different Qualities, (whether Ecclesiasticks or Laicks,) have been decreed lyable to Deposition, or Excommunication, and to be stigmatiz'd and branded with a mark of Infamy.
