Consequences may follow thereupon, what considering Man may not easily apprehend!

If, for determining the Matter of this Enquiry, we will take the Opinion of *Gregory the Great*; he is very confident in concluding the absolute ineffectiveness and invalidity of such a one's Ministery, saying, *Quicunque Sacros Ordines vendunt & emunt, Sacerdotes esse non possunt, Unde Scriptum est, Anathema Danti, Anathema Accipienti, &c.* 'Whosoever sell and buy Holy Orders, cannot be Priests; hence it is that it's written, *Cursed be the Giver, Cursed be the Receiver*: This is Simoniacal Heresie. How then, if they be a Curse, and not Saints themselves, can they sanctifie others? And, seeing they are not in the Body of Christ, how can they either deliver or receive his Body?
