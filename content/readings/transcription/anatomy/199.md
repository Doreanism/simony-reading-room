How hath the climbing up of some, by pecuniary Passages into the Church, opened the mouths of Adversaries, to reproach us as Thieves and Robbers, who enter not in by the Door, nor have any Call from God; and as Hirelings, who serve for bought Benefices? Ah! What a Scandal doth it breed to many Christians? And, how doth it render the Holy Calling contemptible generally amongst the Vulgar, and even amongst those who otherwise have thoughts of Veneration for it, when they see it prostitute to Merchandise, and made a Matter of buying and selling?
