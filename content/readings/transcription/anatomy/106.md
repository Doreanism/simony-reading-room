Cuncta venalia nobis, Templa, Sacerdotes, Altaria, Sacra, &c.

All things are to be sold amongst us, Churches, Priests, Altars, Holy Things, &c. and what not!

And whether it be a light thing to give such occasion of Reproach against God and his Church, those who do so, will find in the day of their Reckoning doubly. 4thly, It is no small sin to expose Sacred and Spiritual Things to contempt, and to give occasion to have them vilified and despised.
