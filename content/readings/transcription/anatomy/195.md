Beware likewise, to advise with those who are of profane and dissolute Lives; these will prove no good Counsellors, for choice of one to the Holy Calling: Such a one will be preferable in their Eyes, whose Conversation differs least from their own; and from whom, they expect least Severity against their Vices.
