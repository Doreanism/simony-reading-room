Thus, when any Person doth purchase a Presentation and Title to a Spiritual Office, or Ecclesiastick Charge; or procures Ordination thereto; or obtains Suffrages for his Election, by Money, or any Gift or Bribe whatsoever, given by himself, or others in his behalf; or, when any Patron bestows a Presentation, or any Bishop, or Ordinary gives Ordination, or any Man gives his Voice for Election to such an Office, upon the condition of receiving any Money, Gift or Bribe; such are in effect guilty of palpable Simony.
