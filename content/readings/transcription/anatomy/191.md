Or, whether you think the Bishop's Sin could excuse yours? Sure I am, you cannot imagine so: For, if you will have none of the Guilt, why will you needs have an hand in the Work? Or tell me, What is the End for which you have this Power? Is it not, that you may improve the same for God's Glory, and the good of Souls? For which, if you have any tenderness or zeal, you will judge your Conscience more concerned, than to be so indifferent in your Choice.
