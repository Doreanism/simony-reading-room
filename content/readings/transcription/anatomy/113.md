Gehazi, and Simon Magus: But, some have set it forth as more hainous than the Heresie of Macedonius, in regard that the Macedonians supposing the Holy Ghost a Creature, made him only a Servant to God. But the Simoniacks, acknowledging the Holy Ghost to be God, yet make him a Servant to themselves, and to their own covetous or ambitious Designs; nay, as their very Slave, they buy, and sell, and make Merchandise of him. And some again have parallel'd it with the Treachery of Judas, in regard, that as he sold and made Merchandise of Jesus Christ the Son of God, so do they of the Holy Ghost, the Spirit of God.
