## CHAP. V.

*Whether they are to be reputed the Ministers of Christ, who enter by Simony?*

WHAT is here proposed is a Case upon the Resolution, whereof dependeth the Satisfaction and Tranquility of the Conscience, both of the Priest and People, of the Pastor and of his Flock, in reference to their respective Concerns in the Administration of the Ministerial Duties.
