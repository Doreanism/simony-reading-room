Secondly, The hainousness of this Sin may appear from the grievous Characters and Evidences of God's Indignation against it, which he gives in the Scriptures. Let us but again reflect upon the Words of the Apostle Peter unto Simon Magus, that first Parent of this Sin in the Christian Church; and upon the account thereof, we find he gives such a Character of him, as might affright any Christian from following his footsteps therein; whilst he tells him, Thy Heart is not right in the sight of God.
