### Fourthly

Consider by what Instinct thou art moved, to design thy self for this Holy Office. Whom God calls, he stirs them up by the secret Instinct of his Blessed Spirit, whereby he works in them such Divine Motions of fervent Charity, and Zeal for his Glory, and the Salvation of Souls, as they are driven thereby to cast themselves upon greatest Hardships, and to resolve to sacrifice their own Ease, Quiet, and carnal Interests, in a Calling wherein they may serve God to these Ends.
