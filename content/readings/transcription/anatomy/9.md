My first Work then must be to shew what *Simony* is. I trust it is known to most who talk thereof, that this sin of *Simony* deriveth its Name from *Simon Magus*; yet must we not therefore suppose, that it had its first beginning from him; but that we read him to have been the first Author thereof in the Christian Church, and was guilty of this sin in an eminent degree; and upon this account it is, that it derives its Denomination from him.
