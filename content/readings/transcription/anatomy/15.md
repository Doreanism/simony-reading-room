And that not only the buying or selling of the Gift of the Holy Ghost, but also of other Sacred and Spiritual things which have affinity therewith, is here condemn'd, and comes within the predicament of Simon's sin, may sufficiently appear from the Text; and that two ways.

First, It may appear from the ground and reason whereupon the Apostle condemns this purpose of Simon Magus, and that was, because he thought that the Gift of God could be purchased with Money.
