All which Practices are nothing else but gross *Simony*, whilst they sell the Truth, and make Merchandise of Souls; whether the Price be the present Profit, or the hope of future Gain, or humane Favour, or Fear, or Ambitious Designs, or Vain-Glory; for all these are the current Money of *Simon Magus*. And of such cheating Merchants in the Church, the Apostles have sufficiently warned us, giving them their proper Characters. The Apostle *Paul* speaking of such, calls them *unruly and vain Talkers, and Deceivers*, ---- teaching *things which they ought not, for filthy Lucre's sake*.
