It is most holy unto the Lord. So that, whosoever transacts by way of buying or selling, for Benefices, or any part of the Church-livings, or Stipends dedicated for the Ministery of Spiritual Offices, cannot shun to incur the Guilt, either of *Simony* or *Sacrilege*.
