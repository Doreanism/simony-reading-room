Mistake me not, *Dear Brethren*, as if it were my design to reproach Young Men, or by reflecting upon others, to purchase an Opinion of the innocency of mine own past Youth, from those Infirmities and Errours which are incident to others; (nay, I desire rather to be humbled in the sight of God, for the least Errour or Irregularity, whereunto I am sensible that the Folly of my Youthful Affections have betray'd me.)
