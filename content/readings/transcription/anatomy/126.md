And therefore, *Dionysius Areopagita* (or whosoever else is the Author of *The Ecclesiastical Hierarchy,*) says of the Person that confers Orders, That he is but Εκφαντορικος, *The Proclaimer,* or, *Declarer of the Divine Election;* 'Who (says he,) by his own Gift, doth not advance those who are Consecrated unto the Honour or Power of Ecclesiastical Order: This he ascribes only to God, whom he calls there Τελεταρχου, *The Prince of Consecration,* or, *chief Ordainer;* and pleads the Authority of Jesus Christ, for ascribing this Power to God alone, and his Holy Spirit.
