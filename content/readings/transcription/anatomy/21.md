Appointment reckoned Spiritual, as being conjoin'd and annexed to Sacred and Spiritual Things, as Ministeries and Helps thereto. Thus things set apart and dedicated to Holy and Spiritual Uses, and for the support and maintainance of Spiritual Offices, with the Rights and Titles thereto, though in their own nature they be things Temporal, yet, in regard of this connexion to Spiritual Things, they become Sacred and Spiritual. And these things God lays claim to, and seals them as his peculiar *Gifts* : Thus the Lord calls the Oblations, Tythes, First-Fruits, and Devoted Things, set apart for the Priests and Levites, *his Gifts* ; saying expresly more than once, *I have given them unto you.*
