For it is received, (as it is said in the Schools,) *Non tanquam merces operis, sed ut stipendium laborantis; non tanquam pretium Spiritualium, sed ut sustentationis stipendium necessarium:* 'Not as the Reward of the Work, but as the wages of the Worker; not as the Price of Spiritual Things, but as the necessary Stipend, for his Sustentation that dispenseth them.
