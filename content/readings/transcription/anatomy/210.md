### Fourthly

It were most necessary, that you should revive somewhat of the ancient Discipline, against Simoniacal Patrons. You know, the ancient Canons did smite with *Anathema*, Lay-Persons, who transacted in such execrable Bargains. And truly, there is nothing could give *Simony* a more mortal Wound, than to bring under your Censures the *Sellers*, as well as the *Buyers* of Spiritual Offices, and to exercise the Spiritual Weapons of the Church equally against both.
