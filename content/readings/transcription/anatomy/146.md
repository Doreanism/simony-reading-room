And therefore says the *Canon Law*, *Simoniacos non Simoniace a Simoniacis Ordinatos, misericorditer per Manus Impositionem, pro temporis necessitate, in officio concedimus permanere:* 'Those whom we account Simoniacks, because of their Ordination by Simoniacal Persons, but have not practised Simony themselves; we do, by the Imposition of Hands, graciously give them leave to continue in their Office, through the necessity of Time.' Whereby it appears, That it was not thought fit to Re-ordain such, but only confirm their former Ordination by Imposition of Hands, without more Rites of Consecration of new again.
