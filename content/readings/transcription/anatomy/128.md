*I you:* And when he had said this, he breathed on them, and saith unto them, *Receive ye the Holy Ghost: Whosefoever's Sins ye remit, they are remitted unto them; and whosesoever's Sins ye retain, they are retained.* And telling them, *Ye shall receive Power, after that the Holy Ghost is come up on you: And ye shall be Witnesses unto me, both in Jerusalem, and in all Judea, and in Samaria, and unto the uttermost Parts of the Earth:* Whereby he meaneth, That they have both the ἐξεσίαν, and the δύναμίν, both the Authority and the Power of their Ministery from the Holy Ghost.
