*que insidias pertimiscamus:* "There's an end of Episcopal Authority, and the Sublime and Divine Power of Governing the Church, if it be come to this pass, that we fear the Threats of evil Men, and the Snares they may lay for us. But, I hope, the fears of these things from Men, will neither move you, to shrink from your Duty, nor to comply with them in Sin; since you know, that your Lord and Master hath so often warned you, *not to fear Men*; and, that you will find much more comfort and quiet in your Conscience, when to please God, you displease Man; than, when by gratifying of Man, you displease God.
