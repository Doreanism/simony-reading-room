Nay, and in hundreds of Texts, the Lord in the Scripture sets this before their Eyes, whom he Calls, and sends forth; thereby to engrave upon their Hearts the sense of the Charge whereof they are to undertake the Burthen, that so they may count the cost before they begin the Work: And therefore, when the Lord told Ananias that had he called Saul, as a chosen Vessel, to bear his Name before the Gentiles, he said withal, I will shew him how great things he must suffer for my Name's Sake. So that, if thou be Called of God, thou wilt have a deep sense of the weight and burthen of the Ministery, upon thy Spirit; and wilt look upon it, as a Work that requires much Watching, and Fasting, and Prayer.
