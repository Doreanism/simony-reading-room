Present them. But pray, Is not the Investing of a Person with a Charge over Souls, the Work of the Lord? Do not you give him a Title to the Charge? And, if you do it without probable grounds of his Ability for the Work, you do all your part to entrust Souls to an insufficient Person; and is not this to do the Work of the Lord *negligently,* or *deceitfully?* (call it as you will.) And what the certification thereof is, you know.
