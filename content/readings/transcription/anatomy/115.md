And thus against Simony do they decree; *Si quis Episcopus, aut Presbyter, aut Diaconus, &c.* If any Bishop, or Presbyter, or Deacon, obtain this Dignity by the help of Money, let him be Deposed; as likewise, he who gave him Ordination, and be separated from the Communion, as Simon Magus was by Peter. More also I might thence cite, but I proceed to what is of more certain Authority.
