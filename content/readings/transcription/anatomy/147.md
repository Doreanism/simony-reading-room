*Secondly,* It being undeniable, that the whole Efficacy of the Ministery depends upon God, and that the Spiritual Effects thereof flow wholly from that Power wherewith he doth inflow and inable Men to Administer in the Mysteries of the Gospel, and from the Concourse of his Holy Spirit with them (For, *The excellency of the Power is of God, and not of Man; Neither is he that planteth any thing, neither he that watereth any thing, but God that giveth the Increase:*) It will then undoubtedly follow, That the external Rites of Ordination can transmit no Virtue or Efficacy into his Ministery who receives the same without the Call, and concurrence of the Holy Ghost, and without any promise of Divine Influence upon his Administrations.
