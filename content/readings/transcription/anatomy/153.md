### *Secondly,*

As for those to whom this Simoniacal Entrance of such a one into the Ministerial Charge is known, and yet by them a ready compliance yielded unto him; not only shall his Administrations prove to such ineffectual; but by their consent and compliance therein, they involve themselves in the guilt of his sinful Practices, whilst thus they have fellowship with the unfruitful work of Darkness, and seeing his Spiritual Theft, do consent unto him; a Sin evidently condemned, both by the Psalmist of Israel, and the Apostle of the Gentiles.
