That buying or selling of any of these things, (save of the last kind) falls under the Sin of *Simony*, is scarce doubted by any. But, as for the Matters of Benefices, and Church-maintainances, and things set apart to serve Spiritual Offices, there be many whose Interest prompts them to cavil upon that Subject: And, because these things are in their own Nature things Temporal, therefore they are bold to argue, That all such things, with their Rights and Presentations, and Titles thereto, may be bought and sold; at least, the buying or selling thereof, falls not under the Guilt of *Simony*.
