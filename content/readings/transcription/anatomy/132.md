And from out of the number of his Ministers, doth our Saviour himself plainly exclude those who come in by Windows, or Posterns, or oblique Passages ; and that under a very bad Character, saying, *Verily, verily, I say unto you, he that entereth not in by the door, into the Sheep-fold, but climbeth up some other way, the same is a Thief, and a Robber.* And I'm sure, none dare say, That they who climb up by the Ladder of Simony, do enter in by the Door.
