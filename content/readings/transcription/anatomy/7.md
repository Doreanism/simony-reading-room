Wherefore, how long that Spirit of Christ, which made the zeal of God's House to eat him up, possessed the Governours of the Church, it moved them to make very strict and cautious Laws and Canons for stopping all those Conduits whereby this Corruption might be fear'd to enter, and to inflict severe Censures upon such as they found guilty of this sin. But when that Primitive Fervour began to decay, and the Spirit of the World to take hold of the luke-warm Clergy, then began *Simon Magus* again to set up the Head, being animated by the secret Favour of a strong Party in the Church, of whom (though he knew they would not avowedly own his Interest,) he was confident, they would prove clandestine Abettors of his Designs.
