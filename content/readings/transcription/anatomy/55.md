But though the Person for whom one interceeds, be never so worthy; yet if his Intercessions for him, proceed not from a Religious, but only from a carnal Principle, to promote a worldly Design; then is the Intercessour guilty of this Simony by the Tongue, whilst his End therein is not to serve the Lord Jesus Christ, but only a carnal Interest.
