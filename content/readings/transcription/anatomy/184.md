But seeing you are in Possession thereof, I would warn you to look upon it as a Matter of great Trust, for which you must be accountable to the great God of Heaven and Earth: For, thereby the Vineyard of God comes under your Charge, to look out for faithful Labourers to put into it; thereby you are entrusted with the Souls of People, to look out for faithful Pastors to commit them to.
