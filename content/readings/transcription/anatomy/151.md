### *First,*

To those who know a Pastor to have conveyed himself in upon them by Simoniacal Means, and are griev'd that they should be given over to such a hopeless Ministry, I say, it highly concerns them, to search into the Cause of God's Controversie with them; and it is like, they shall find it to be such as provoked God of old, to give over his People to the like Judgment, to the seduction of false and treacherous Prophets, and of covetous and careless, cruel and foolish Shepherds and Watchmen; to wit, because they despised his Word, and were disobedient and rebellious to the Voice of his faithful Messengers; as may appear from *Micah*, *Zephaniah*; *Zechariah*, and others of the Prophets.
