And sure, whosoever is touched with this sense, and seriously considereth what a weighty Charge it is, will never make use of the Silver-keys of *Simon Magus*, to open a Passage for himself thereto; nay, save upon the account of the Obligation of a Call from God, he would not stoop to take it up. It is only a delusory Fancy of carnal Minds, that, looking upon it as an Office destin'd for Ease, and Pleasure, and Profit, makes Men run to purchase it with Money.
