But whatsoever the Price be, alas, how unhappy Merchants are you! And, what a poor Purchase make you! If you do it to gratifie Man, your Gain can in no wise countervail your Damage, whilst you lose the Favour of the Creator, to conciliate the Favour of the Creature, who, 'tis like, at last will requite you as the Elders of the *Jews* did *Judas*; and when your Conscience, distracted with the Horrour of your Guilt, makes you cry out, *We have sinned in betraying innocent*
