3dly, With good Reason I may say, That whosoever buys or sells a Benefice, or Presentation thereto, buys and sells the Office also: For it is a known Maxim, Caufa caufa, eft caufa caufati: "That which is the "Cause of the Cause, is the Cause "also of the Effect. Now the Money which the Buyer pays to the Seller, is the Cause which procures the Presentation; the Presentation procures Ordination, and Ordination the Office; and so the Money answereth all; and being the Cause of the Presentation, is the Cause also of the Office, which without the Presentation is not attain'd.
