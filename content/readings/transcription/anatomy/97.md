Such a one, is indeed but a base Simoniacal Pedler, making Merchandise of the Gifts of God. But such is not he, who requires but his Allowance of these due Accommodations which the diligent Attendance to his Duties call for, wherein he exerciseth himself without any base design, giving unto every one their portion in due season; nay, this is the faithful and wise Steward; and blessed shall he be, when at the coming of his Lord, he shall be found so doing.
