God and Man, with the Blessed Apostle Paul, *We are not as many, which corrupt the Word of God; but as of Sincerity, but as of God, in the sight of God speak we in Christ. And our Exhortation was not of Deceit, nor of Uncleanness, nor in Guile; But as we were allowed of God to be put in trust with the Gospel, even so we speak; not as pleasing Men, but God, which tryeth our Hearts: For neither at any time used we flattering Words, as ye know, nor a Cloak of Covetousness, God is witness: Nor of Men sought we Glory.*
