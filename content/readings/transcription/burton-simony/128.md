So shall you bring a blessing vpon you and yours, yea vpon this whole Land and Church, extending euen to Posteritie, whose race shall (we trust) sing the memorable Acts of this euery way (as we pray) most prosperous and happy Parliament: and your humble Suppliant shall daily pray to God so to blesse this your happy Assembly, as hereby Gods glory may be aduanced, Religion propagated, the Common-wealth established, Antichristian Heresie extirpated, and your selues blessed in your deed, Amen.
