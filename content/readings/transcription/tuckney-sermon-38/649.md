1. Hard to be hit on, and therefore the more carefully to be heeded. Much good lost because the opportunity is slipt : because to every purpose there is a time and judgment, therefore the misery of Man is great upon him, Ecclef. 8. 6.

2. Most Successful usually, if observed. The thing is then Beautiful, Ecclef. 3. 11. and therefore taking : upon the Wheels them and therefore goeth on smoothly. I am no Arminian, to make the Efficacy of Grace to consist in Moral perswasions applied tempore & modo congruis, in the fitness and Seasonableness of such Applications. No : I know that God to shew the freeness and prevailing Efficacy of his Grace, sometimes works it (as in Paul and others) when the Sinner is most indisposed, as Christ the Physician came when the World was most Corrupted ; who, otherwise than other Physicians, can to purpose give Physick in the height of a Fit, as he cured Simon's Wife's Mother, In aestu Febris, in the very Paroxism of her Disease. Nullum tempus occurrit Regi.
