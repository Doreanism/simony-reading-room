The complete text of the anonymous 1700 treatise on simony, comprising twelve chapters examining the nature of simony, its manifestations in the Church of England's patronage system, and remedies for it. The work addresses bishops and clergy, drawing on scripture and patristic sources to argue that simoniacal corruption destroys the whole fabric of the Church.
