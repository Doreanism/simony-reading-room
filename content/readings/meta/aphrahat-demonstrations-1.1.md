The opening paragraph of Aphrahat's first Demonstration (*De Fide*), addressed to the unnamed correspondent who wrote requesting instruction in faith. Aphrahat acknowledges the letter and cites Matthew 10:8 — "Freely you have received, freely give" — to justify his willingness to share what he himself has received without charge. The same verse and theme are taken up at length in Demonstration X §8 (cross-referenced in the footnote here).
