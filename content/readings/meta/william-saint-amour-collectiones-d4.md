Signs forty through forty-eight from the fourth distinction of William of Saint-Amour's *Collectiones*, part of the catalogue of scriptural marks by which pseudo-preachers may be distinguished from true apostles. These signs concern motives of gain, manner of receiving offerings, and related abuses.
