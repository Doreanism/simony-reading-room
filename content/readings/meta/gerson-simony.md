Jean Gerson's treatise on simony, a short scholastic work laying out suppositions, propositions, and considerations on simoniacal corruption in ecclesiastical appointments and benefices. Printed in the 1706 Antwerp Opera Omnia, vol. II, running from column 645 to column 652.
