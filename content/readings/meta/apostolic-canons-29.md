The twenty-ninth of the *Canones Apostolorum*, the collection of eighty-five disciplinary canons appended to Book VIII of the *Constitutiones Apostolorum* (c. 380). Presented in Funk's edition with the Greek text on the facing page. Canon 29 forbids the purchase of episcopal, presbyteral, or diaconal office: anyone who obtains ordination through money is to be deposed, along with the bishop who ordained him, and both are to be cut off entirely from communion — "as Simon Magus was by me, Peter."
