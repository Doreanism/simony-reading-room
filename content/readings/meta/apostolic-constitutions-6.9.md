Chapter 9 of Book VI of the *Constitutiones Apostolorum* (c. 380), presented in Funk's critical edition with the Greek text. Peter narrates his encounters with Simon Magus — first at Caesarea, then at Rome, where Simon attempts to fly before Nero, falls, and breaks his legs. One of the principal patristic retellings of the Simon Magus legend.
