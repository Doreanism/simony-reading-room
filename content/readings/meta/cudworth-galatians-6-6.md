Ralph Cudworth's commentary on Galatians 6:6 — "Let him that is taught in the word, make him that hath taught him partaker of all his goods" — from his continuation of Perkins's exposition of the Epistle. The section treats the verse as an apostolic rule for the maintenance of the ministry, examining the meanings of "taught in the word," "him that teacheth," and "communicate." It argues from scripture, natural law, and civil law that congregations are bound to provide a sufficient and liberal allowance for their ministers, condemning those who withhold support or strip the Church of her revenues.
